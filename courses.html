CSE - Computer Science Engineering

Duration: 3 Years Diploma

The CSE department provides strong knowledge in programming, artificial intelligence, data science, and software development.

Subjects:

  • Data Structures
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Web Technologies

Career Opportunities:

Software Developer, Web Developer, Data Analyst, System Engineer.

ECE - Electronics and Communication Engineering

Duration: 3 Years Diploma

ECE focuses on communication systems, embedded systems, and electronic circuit design.

Subjects:

  • Digital Electronics
  • Analog Electronics
  • Microprocessors
  • Communication Systems
  • Embedded Systems

Career Opportunities:

Electronics Engineer, Telecom Engineer, Embedded Developer.

EEE - Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Duration: 3 Years Diploma

EEE deals with electrical machines, power generation, transmission and control systems.

Subjects:

  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Power Electronics

Career Opportunities:

Electrical Engineer, Power Plant Engineer, Maintenance Engineer.

MECH - Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 3 Years Diploma

Mechanical Engineering focuses on machines, manufacturing, thermal systems and automobile engineering.

Subjects:

  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Technology

Career Opportunities:

Mechanical Engineer, Production Engineer, Automobile Engineer.
