Alumni Association

The Alumni Association of Government Polytechnic for Women, Warangal maintains a strong connection between the institution and its former students. Our alumni are successfully working in various government and private sectors.

Objectives:

  • To maintain a lifelong relationship with former students.
  • To support current students through guidance and mentorship.
  • To organize alumni meets and networking events.
  • To help students with placements and career advice.

Our Alumni Achievements:

  • Working in reputed IT companies.
  • Government Engineers in Electrical and Civil Departments.
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners.
  • Higher education in B.Tech and M.Tech.

Activities Conducted:

  • Annual Alumni Meet
  • Guest Lectures by Alumni
  • Career Guidance Programs
  • Placement Support Sessions
