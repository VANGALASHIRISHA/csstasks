About College

Government Polytechnic for Women, Warangal, an epitome of excellence, was established in the year 1980 and located in the heart of Warangal city. This institution is certified by ISO 9001:2015 for providing best technical educational services and accrediated by National Board of Accrediation till 2026 for offering quality outcome based education

Our Moto

To achieve academic excellence by imparting quality and value-based education. To create a pool of efficient technocrats by providing employability skills for the betterment of society. To strengthen the students for pursuing higher education in their relevant fields.

Our vision

Our qualified and dedicated faculty form a strong support system to fulfill the mission of our institution by quickly responding to the changes happening in the learning environment and successfully transforming themselves to the digitized, virtual environment.
